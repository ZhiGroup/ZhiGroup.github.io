Zhi Lab advances precision medicine through computational genomics, pangenomics, clinical foundation models, and AI-powered imaging genetics. We develop scalable algorithms and deep learning systems for biobank-scale genomic data, longitudinal electronic health records, and imaging-derived phenotypes. In July 2026, Dr. Degui Zhi will join Yale University as Professor in the Department of Biomedical Informatics and Data Science and Director of Bioinformatics.
